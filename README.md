# Collaborative Drawing App

Focuses on concurrent modifications of the art on the whiteboard, useful for team brainstorming or project collaboration

1. Open https://collaborative-drawing-app.vercel.app/ and enter your display name  
2. Work on the project, select and test various things like color picker, eraser, rainbow and others like undo, redo, clear  
3. Copy the invite link on the left side bottom and send to your friend or just use another tab 
4. **(Split view both tabs to check concurrency, cool feature!!)** Your pointer drawing will be shown with your name to the other user, likewise similarly his drawing is also shown by a pointer with his name on it  
5. <img width="1864" height="1079" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b395c92c-a23e-4d64-a88e-a2d178a4d22a" />

6. In the end after the completion go to the top right and click on save as JPEG, the canvas will be downloaded in your local system
